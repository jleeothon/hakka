== Possessive

|===
| 2+| Sg. 2+| Pl.

h| 1st | ⺅厓? | ngá | ⺅厓哋嘅 | ngá dí
h| 2nd | 你? | nyá |  你哋嘅 | nyá dí
h| 3rd | 佢? | giá |  佢哋嘅 | kiá dí
|===

For possessive pronouns, more often, a counting word may be attached to the
personal pronoun instead of 嘅.
